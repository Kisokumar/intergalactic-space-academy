About:

- The spaceships use thrust vectoring! Make sure you adjust the angle at which you fire the booster!

- Remember the faster you're going, the more damage meteors will do to your ship!

- Collect the stars (they give you more points and they contain super fuel!).

- Avoid colliding with the meteors & try to take the least amount of damage.

- Get the highest score you can before your fuel runs out and you're stranded in deep space!

Controls:

PC (↑) Thruster

The main propellant of your spaceship.

PC (← →) Side Boosters

Use this to rotate your spaceship and to counter steer when meteors knock you off trajectory.

PC (X) Warp Drive

Booster speed increased 3X! Use wisely as this will deplete fuel tank faster!

PC (Z) Teleport

For when you're caught in a heavy meteor shower (beware you may teleport into a worse situation)

PC (R) Reset

Restart to original game state.
